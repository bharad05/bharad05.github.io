Research Interests

Work

Projects

California 5th Climate Change Assessment

This project focuses on characterising drought risk and uncertainty for California's state-level climate adaptation planning.

Water Quality Trading as a Risk Management Instrument

This project developed a mathematical optimization model in GAMS to simulate nutrient loading and evaluate water quality trading as a risk management instrument.

Nutrient Neutrality & Urban Development Trade-offs

This project integrated housing demand forecasts with nutrient risk constraints to model development scenarios.

Bumbu Watershed Water Quality — Papua New Guinea

Water quality monitoring and phytoremediation assessment for watershed-scale risk mitigation.
